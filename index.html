/// How a batch runs

From first boot to final certification.

WEEK 1

Foundations on the bench

Assemble and diagnose a PC, terminate your first cable, and set up a working LAN by the end of week one.

WEEK 3–8

Core track training

Deep-dive into your chosen track — CCNA routing labs, RHEL shell administration, or cloud console work — with weekly graded labs.

WEEK 9–10

Project & mock exam

Build a capstone project mirroring a real deployment, then sit a full-length mock certification exam.

WEEK 11+

Certification & placement

Sit your official certification exam and move into interview preparation with our placement cell.
